Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Quality

M&C Enterprise is a proudly South African construction and services company based in Polokwane, Limpopo. Founded on the principles of integrity, quality craftsmanship, and community upliftment, we have grown into one of the most trusted construction partners in the region.

We serve homeowners, businesses, property developers, schools, and government departments across Limpopo — delivering every project with the same commitment to excellence regardless of size or budget.

🏅
CIDB Registered
Fully registered with the Construction Industry Development Board, qualifying us for government and public sector contracts.
🤝
B-BBEE Compliant
Proudly compliant with South Africa's Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment framework — supporting transformation.
📋
Guaranteed Workmanship
Every project comes with a written workmanship guarantee. We stand behind every brick, tile, and coat of paint.
📍
Locally Rooted
Based in Polokwane with deep roots in the Limpopo community. We hire locally and invest in the communities we serve.
